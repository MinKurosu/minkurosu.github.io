Stuff happens which leads to me going to a very busy bank. It turns into an airport. I get on a plane with Kato. I'm about to pass out when I notice the flight attendant is actually a giant alien and the inside of the plane is made out of flesh. Obviously, I'm shocked and wonder if nobody else is seeing this. They aren't, only Kato and I can because I accidentally tripped up the perception filter. The perception filter is a giant eyeball embedded into the flesh wall with a flap of skin covering it. The flap shifted, making the eye visible so the filter turned off. Then I realise the reason she collected everyone's phones in a giant bin bag is because she is a shapeshifter who takes on the qualities of whatever she touches. The gas that was making me lightheaded has knocked everyone else out. She starts momologuing about how public transport is one of humanity's greatest achievements and uses the secret railways saving Jewish people in WW2 as an example. She reveals she wants to take ALL public transport to her home planet because there is "so much food all in one place." Then the dream shifts and shows the flight attendant as an adolescent. She is black instead of white now and has a girlfriend who she loves very much. She writes poetry bout her and lives an idyllic life until the girlfriend walks out on her because the flight attendant can't be physically affectionate due to her shapeshifting. She tries to go after her girlfriend  but is slowed down because there’s a lot of cars when she’s trying to cross the street.
